The Satan Cube

Joseph Rutland
The Satan Cube: How far would you go to save the life of a loved one? Noah Atherton (Played by Joseph Rutland) is an online cuber with a minor cult following, and is willing to risk a deal with the devil, "
 
Joseph has recently written, directed and starred in his new short film 'The Satan Cube'. The story of Noah Atherton, a disturbed Cube-Tuber who dabbles in the dark arts to save his ailing mother... and ends up sacrificing everything.
 
Filming took place in summer 2022, and involved multiple actors, technicians, and other talented artists.
 
 
Many thanks to everyone who made this short film possible.
