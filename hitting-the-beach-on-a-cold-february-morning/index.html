February 28, 2021

Hitting the beach on a cold February morning.

Joseph Rutland

With the third lockdown nearly at an end, I felt secure enough to take my daily, government-sanctioned exercise at New Brighton promenade. There were quite a few people around but not too many.

Note my colour coordinated mask, jumper, and jacket.

Am I really that stylish? Yes I am.

Here’s hoping we’re back to normal soon.

I was hoping to go on the actual sands, however I didn’t consult the tide timetable before setting out. While there is a beach at high tide, it’s 20ft underwater.
