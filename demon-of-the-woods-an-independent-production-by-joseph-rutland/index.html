Demon of the woods - an independent production by Joseph Rutland 

Joseph Rutland
I have finally finished the short movie Demon of the Woods I wrote, directed and starred in the short horror, alongside other actors who I cast. The film took two years to complete due to COVID-19 although you wouldn’t be able to tell (apart for the actors randomly aging and de-aging between 16 and 18)!
 
I also created all of the practical effects for this film, including a fork stabbed through the back of a characters neck and a characters face that had been smashed to pieces by a rock wielding madman.
