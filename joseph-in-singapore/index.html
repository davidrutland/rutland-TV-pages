Joseph In Singapore

Joseph Rutland

English weather in a tropical climate. Joseph stayed in the 'Little India' section of the City and went on many misadventures. On his first day he tried to walk as far as he could but got trapped by rain so HAD to watch the Attack on Titan movie in cinemas.

On another occasion he spent the whole night in an Indian bar where he danced with everybody before retiring to his hospital where the man underneath him was dying from some ailment (he survived though).

On the final day, Christian finally arrived and they arranged to meet by the 'Merlion Statue' Joseph was pleased that it was walkable so set off. After a while he noticed his journey was getting steeper and steeper and more and more jungle-y. About 3/4 of the way there he realised that he had accidentally entered the rainforest. When he arrived at the statue (which was at the highest point in Singapore) it turned out that Christian meant a completely different statue.

After the confusion was settled, Joseph and Christian headed of to an Indian Cow festival where they got special cow milk which they mixed intoÂ  a terrible chicken tikka they ordered. It was not tasty.

Joseph enjoyed his time in Singapore but it was far too expensive for him. Could his wallet survive this again? Probably not.
