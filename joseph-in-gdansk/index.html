Joseph In Gdansk

Joseph Rutland

Joseph has recently been trecking around Western Poland with his Girlfrend. Stranded outside on the first night, they had to seek refuge in a Blues Brothers themed bar.

He ventured from Gdansk to Sopot (Polish Southport, complete with a nice curry) and the cold fishing village of Hel where he trecked through old German forts and trenches.
