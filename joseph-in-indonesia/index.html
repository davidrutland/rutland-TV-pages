Joseph in Jakarta

Joseph Rutland

In the last leg of my trip, Joseph went to Indonesia. Possibly the best accomodation in Asia he's ever experienced. On the second day, Joseph and Christian went to the beach where a bunch of Indonesian kids kept asking questions such as 'Do you like Apples?' 'Do you like Cats?' and various other flavours of that question. Joseph also had the worst fish and chips of his life on that beach. 

He and Christian then ventured (On a guided tour) to a tea plantation and an active volacano. Volcanoes stink, they are absolutely aweful on the nose. Joseph nearly bought a blow-dart from a volcano woman and he's glad he didn't because they're illegal in the UK (Who knew). Afterwards, he and Christian retired to a Volcanic spar where they got their feet nice and massaged.

Unfortunately, this was to be the last night before Christian headed back to Finland, so they grabbed a random Indonesian man called Ricky and went to a swinging 20's themed jazz/pizza bar and the three of them had a wonderful candlelit dinner.
