Joseph in Istanbul

Joseph Rutland

Joseph is a prolific lover of all things Turkish, so it utterly delighted him to finally visit the place. He got by with the small ammount of Turkish he knew. 

However, the most exciting thing for him was the pre-arranged meeting with artist 'C.M.Kosemen' author of 'All Tommorows' which Joseph had arranged. They talked for hours whilst Kosemen created art for Joseph's family and freinds. 

This was such an amazing city, Joseph wishes he could've stayed there forever.
